EVENTUS CDD RULESET (v1)
Client Type × Risk Level
Status: Authoritative internal document
Derived from: Eventus AML Policies & Procedures (PCPs)
Applies to: Client and Matter Level Risk Assessments (Stage 1)
Version: v1
Scope: Individuals and UK private limited companies only
This ruleset provides a deterministic mapping of client type and assessed risk level to mandatory Customer Due Diligence (CDD), Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD), Source of Wealth (SoW) and Source of Funds (SoF) actions.
This ruleset does not introduce new obligations. It operationalises existing requirements in the Eventus AML Policies & Procedures, the Eventus PWRA, and the Money Laundering Regulations 2017.
The Assistant must apply this ruleset exactly as written. No discretionary or conditional language may be used.

GENERAL RULES (APPLY TO ALL BLOCKS)
1. All individuals required to be verified must be verified using either:
o Amiqus electronic ID&V; or
o non-electronic verification consisting of:
* one government-issued photo ID (valid passport or UK photocard driving licence); and
* one proof of address (utility bill, bank or building society statement, or council tax statement dated within the last 3 months).
(Eventus AML PCPs, §1.1; MLR 2017, regs. 28(4), 28(18))
2. All CDD actions set out below are mandatory outcomes, not options.
3. Where EDD is stated as required, MLRO approval is mandatory before the matter proceeds.
(Eventus AML PCPs, §20; MLR 2017, reg. 33)

PART A — INDIVIDUAL CLIENTS

A1. Individual Client — LOW RISK
Mandatory CDD Actions
? Identify the client by recording:
* full legal name
* date of birth
* residential address
? Verify the client’s identity using one approved verification method (see General Rules).
? Obtain and retain information on:
* the nature and purpose of the matter.
? Ongoing monitoring to be applied throughout the retainer.
EDD / SoW / SoF
? Enhanced Due Diligence: NOT required
? Source of Wealth: NOT required
? Source of Funds: NOT required
Authority:
(Eventus AML PCPs, §§7, 17; MLR 2017, reg. 28)

A2. Individual Client — MEDIUM RISK
Mandatory CDD Actions
? Identify the client by recording:
* full legal name
* date of birth
* residential address
? Verify the client’s identity using one approved verification method.
? Obtain and record information on:
* the nature and purpose of the matter.
? Apply ongoing monitoring throughout the retainer.
Source of Wealth
? Source of Wealth Form must be completed and retained.
? Obtain supporting evidence aligned to the declared source of wealth, including one or more of:
* payslips or tax returns (employment income);
* bank statements (savings);
* sale documentation (asset or business sale);
* inheritance documentation (where applicable).
Source of Funds
? Source of Funds Form must be completed and retained.
? Obtain bank statement(s) evidencing:
* origin of funds; and
* availability of funds for the transaction.
EDD
? Enhanced Due Diligence: NOT required
Authority:
(Eventus AML PCPs, §§7, 8, 11, 17; MLR 2017, reg. 28)

A3. Individual Client — HIGH RISK
Mandatory CDD Actions
? All actions listed for Medium Risk individuals.
Enhanced Due Diligence
? Enhanced Due Diligence REQUIRED, including:
* senior management / MLRO approval;
* enhanced scrutiny of Source of Wealth and Source of Funds;
* corroborative documentation where available.
? Apply enhanced ongoing monitoring.
Authority:
(Eventus AML PCPs, §20; MLR 2017, reg. 33; LSAG 2025, §5.15)

PART B — UK PRIVATE LIMITED COMPANIES

B1. UK Private Limited Company — LOW RISK
Entity Identification
? Obtain and retain:
* certificate of incorporation;
* articles of association.
? Carry out and retain a Companies House search showing:
* registered name and number;
* registered office;
* current directors;
* PSC register.
? Record confirmation that Companies House information is consistent with client-provided information.
Directors
? Identify all directors by recording:
* full legal name;
* date of birth;
* service address;
* role as director.
? Verify the identity of each director using one approved verification method.
Beneficial Ownership
? Identify all individuals who:
* hold more than 25% of shares or voting rights; or
* otherwise exercise control.
? Verify the identity of each beneficial owner using one approved verification method.
Ongoing Monitoring
? Apply ongoing monitoring throughout the retainer.
EDD / SoW / SoF
? Enhanced Due Diligence: NOT required
? Source of Wealth: NOT required
? Source of Funds: NOT required
Authority:
(Eventus AML PCPs, §7; MLR 2017, regs. 28, 30)

B2. UK Private Limited Company — MEDIUM RISK
Mandatory CDD Actions
? All actions listed for Low Risk UK private limited companies.
Ownership and Control
? Obtain and retain an ownership and control structure chart.
? Record confirmation that ownership and control are understood and not opaque.
Source of Wealth
? Source of Wealth Form must be completed and retained.
? Obtain supporting evidence aligned to the declared source, including one or more of:
* statutory accounts;
* dividend vouchers;
* business sale documentation;
* loan agreements (where applicable).
Source of Funds
? Source of Funds Form must be completed and retained.
? Obtain bank statement(s) evidencing:
* origin of funds; and
* availability of funds for the transaction.
EDD
? Enhanced Due Diligence: NOT required
Authority:
(Eventus AML PCPs, §§7, 8, 11, 17; MLR 2017, reg. 28)

B3. UK Private Limited Company — HIGH RISK
Mandatory CDD Actions
? All actions listed for Medium Risk UK private limited companies.
Enhanced Due Diligence
? Enhanced Due Diligence REQUIRED, including:
* MLRO approval prior to proceeding;
* enhanced scrutiny of ownership and control;
* enhanced scrutiny of Source of Wealth and Source of Funds;
* corroborative documentation where available.
? Apply enhanced ongoing monitoring.
Authority:
(Eventus AML PCPs, §20; MLR 2017, reg. 33; LSAG 2025, §5.15)

EXCLUSIONS (v1)
This ruleset does not apply to:
* trusts;
* charities or not-for-profit organisations;
* overseas companies;
* entities listed on regulated markets;
* any client type not covered by the Individual or UK Private Limited Company CMLRAs.
Such matters must be assessed directly by reference to the Eventus AML PCPs and escalated to the MLRO where required.

UK LLPs and UK Partnerships — Client Type × Risk Level
Status: Authoritative internal document
Derived from: Eventus AML Policies & Procedures (PCPs)
Applies to: Client and Matter Level Risk Assessments (Stage 1)
Version: v2
Scope:
* UK Limited Liability Partnerships (LLPs)
* UK Partnerships (including general and limited partnerships)
This ruleset provides a deterministic mapping of client type and assessed risk level to mandatory CDD, EDD, Source of Wealth and Source of Funds actions for LLPs and partnerships.
This ruleset does not introduce new obligations. It operationalises existing requirements in the Eventus AML Policies & Procedures, the Eventus PWRA, and the Money Laundering Regulations 2017.
The Assistant must apply this ruleset exactly as written.

GENERAL RULES (APPLY TO ALL BLOCKS)
1. All individuals required to be verified must be verified using either:
o Amiqus electronic ID&V; or
o non-electronic verification consisting of:
* one government-issued photo ID (valid passport or UK photocard driving licence); and
* one proof of address (utility bill, bank or building society statement, or council tax statement dated within the last 3 months).
(Eventus AML PCPs, §1.1; MLR 2017, regs. 28(4), 28(18))
2. “Members” (LLPs) and “partners” (partnerships) are treated as individuals acting on behalf of the client and must be identified and verified unless an exemption applies.
3. Where EDD is stated as required, MLRO approval is mandatory before the matter proceeds.
(Eventus AML PCPs, §20; MLR 2017, reg. 33)

PART C — UK LLPs AND UK PARTNERSHIPS

C1. UK LLP / Partnership — LOW RISK
Entity Identification
? Obtain and retain:
* LLP incorporation certificate or partnership agreement;
* constitutional or governing documents (where applicable).
? Carry out and retain a Companies House search (LLP) or equivalent reliable source (partnership) showing:
* registered name and number (if applicable);
* registered or principal place of business;
* members or partners.
? Record confirmation that public record information is consistent with client-provided information.
Members / Partners — Identification and Verification
? Identify all members or partners by recording:
* full legal name;
* date of birth;
* residential or service address;
* role as member or partner.
? Verify the identity of each member or partner using one approved verification method.
Beneficial Ownership
? Confirm that the members or partners identified constitute the beneficial owners of the LLP or partnership.
? Where any individual exercises control by other means, identify and verify that individual.
Ongoing Monitoring
? Apply ongoing monitoring throughout the retainer.
EDD / SoW / SoF
? Enhanced Due Diligence: NOT required
? Source of Wealth: NOT required
? Source of Funds: NOT required
Authority:
(Eventus AML PCPs, §7; MLR 2017, regs. 28, 30; LSAG 2025, §6.14)

C2. UK LLP / Partnership — MEDIUM RISK
Mandatory CDD Actions
? All actions listed for Low Risk UK LLPs / Partnerships.
Ownership and Control
? Obtain and retain a clear description or diagram of:
* ownership; and
* management / control arrangements.
? Record confirmation that ownership and control are understood and not opaque.
Source of Wealth
? Source of Wealth Form must be completed and retained.
? Obtain supporting evidence aligned to the declared source, including one or more of:
* partnership accounts;
* profit share or capital contribution records;
* business sale documentation;
* loan agreements (where applicable).
Source of Funds
? Source of Funds Form must be completed and retained.
? Obtain bank statement(s) evidencing:
* origin of funds; and
* availability of funds for the transaction.
EDD
? Enhanced Due Diligence: NOT required
Authority:
(Eventus AML PCPs, §§7, 8, 11, 17; MLR 2017, reg. 28)

C3. UK LLP / Partnership — HIGH RISK
Mandatory CDD Actions
? All actions listed for Medium Risk UK LLPs / Partnerships.
Enhanced Due Diligence
? Enhanced Due Diligence REQUIRED, including:
* MLRO approval prior to proceeding;
* enhanced scrutiny of ownership and control;
* enhanced scrutiny of Source of Wealth and Source of Funds;
* corroborative documentation where available.
? Apply enhanced ongoing monitoring.
Authority:
(Eventus AML PCPs, §20; MLR 2017, reg. 33; LSAG 2025, §5.15)

EXCLUSIONS (v2)
This ruleset does not apply to:
* overseas LLPs or partnerships;
* LLPs or partnerships with trust or nominee partners;
* professional partnerships operating in high-risk jurisdictions;
* any structure requiring bespoke MLRO assessment.
Such matters must be assessed directly under the Eventus AML PCPs and escalated to the MLRO.
