327 Concealing etc
(1)A person commits an offence if he—
(a)conceals criminal property;
(b)disguises criminal property;

(2F)A person (“P”) who does an act mentioned in paragraph (c), (d) or (e) of subsection (1)

(c)at the time of the act, P knows or suspects that part but not all of the funds in
